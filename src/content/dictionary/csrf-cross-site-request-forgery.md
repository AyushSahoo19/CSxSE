---
term: "CSRF (Cross-Site Request Forgery)"
category: "Security"
level: "Intermediate"
---

An attack that tricks an authenticated user's browser into making unintended requests to a site where they're logged in.

### Example

You're logged into your bank. You visit a malicious page with a hidden form that submits 'transfer $10000 to attacker'. Your browser includes your bank cookies automatically.
