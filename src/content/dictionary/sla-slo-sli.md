---
term: "SLA / SLO / SLI"
category: "DevOps & Infrastructure"
level: "Intermediate"
---

SLA: contractual agreement with customers (99.9% uptime). SLO: internal target (99.95%). SLI: actual measured metric (99.93% this month).

### Example

SLA to customers: 99.9% uptime (≤8.7 hours downtime/year). Internal SLO: 99.95%. SLI shows you're at 99.93% — within SLA but below SLO. Time to improve.
