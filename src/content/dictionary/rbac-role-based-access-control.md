---
term: "RBAC (Role-Based Access Control)"
category: "Security"
level: "Intermediate"
---

Assigning permissions to roles, then assigning roles to users. Users inherit permissions from their roles.

### Example

Roles: Admin (full access), Editor (read/write), Viewer (read-only). User Alice gets 'Editor' role → can read and write but can't delete or manage users.
