---
term: "Semantic Versioning (SemVer)"
category: "Software Engineering Process"
level: "Intermediate"
---

Version format: MAJOR.MINOR.PATCH (e.g., 2.4.1). MAJOR: breaking changes. MINOR: new features, backward-compatible. PATCH: bug fixes.

### Example

Library goes from 2.4.1 to 3.0.0 — expect breaking changes, update carefully. From 2.4.1 to 2.5.0 — new features, safe to upgrade.
