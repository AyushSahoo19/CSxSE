---
term: "Time-Series Database"
category: "Databases & Storage"
level: "Advanced"
---

Optimized for time-stamped data: metrics, events, sensor readings. Efficient at storing, compressing, and querying sequential time data.

### Example

InfluxDB, TimescaleDB — storing server CPU metrics every second for a year. Standard RDBMS would struggle; TSDB handles billions of points efficiently.
