term: "Junior / Mid / Senior / Staff / Principal Engineer"
category: "Career & Professional"
level: "Foundational"
---

Career levels in software engineering. Junior: learning, guided work. Mid: independent contributor. Senior: leads projects, mentors. Staff/Principal: org-wide technical leadership.

### Example

Junior: implements assigned tasks with guidance. Senior: designs systems, mentors juniors, unblocks the team. Staff: defines technical strategy across multiple teams.
