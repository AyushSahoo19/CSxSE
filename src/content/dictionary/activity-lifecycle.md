---
term: "Activity Lifecycle"
category: "Mobile Development"
level: "Intermediate"
---

The set of states an Android Activity goes through from the time it's created until it's destroyed. Key callbacks include onCreate, onStart, onResume, onPause, onStop, and onDestroy.

### Example

Using the `onPause` method to halt a video's playback when a user receives a phone call or switches to another app.
