---
term: "Webhook vs Polling"
category: "Backend Engineering"
level: "Intermediate"
---

Polling: client repeatedly asks 'any updates?' (wasteful). Webhook: server pushes notification to client when something happens (efficient).

### Example

Polling: checking your mailbox every 5 minutes. Webhook: a doorbell that rings when mail arrives. Webhooks are more efficient for infrequent events.
