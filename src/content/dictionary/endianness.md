---
term: "Endianness"
category: "Fundamentals"
level: "Advanced"
---

The order in which bytes of a multi-byte value are stored in memory. Big-endian: most significant byte first. Little-endian: least significant first.

### Example

The number 0x12345678 stored as 12 34 56 78 (big-endian) vs 78 56 34 12 (little-endian). Matters in networking and cross-platform work.
