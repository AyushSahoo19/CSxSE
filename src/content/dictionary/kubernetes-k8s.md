---
term: "Kubernetes (K8s)"
category: "DevOps & Infrastructure"
level: "Advanced"
---

An orchestration platform for managing containerized applications at scale. Handles deployment, scaling, load balancing, self-healing, and rolling updates.

### Example

You tell K8s: 'Run 5 instances of my web app.' If one crashes, K8s automatically starts a replacement. Traffic spikes? K8s scales to 20 instances.
