---
term: "NoSQL"
category: "Databases & Storage"
level: "Intermediate"
---

Non-relational databases designed for specific data models and flexible schemas. Types: document (MongoDB), key-value (Redis), columnar (Cassandra), graph (Neo4j).

### Example

A product catalog where each product has different attributes — one has 'size', another has 'wattage'. MongoDB's flexible schema handles this naturally.
