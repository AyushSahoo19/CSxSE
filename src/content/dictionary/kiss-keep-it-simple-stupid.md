---
term: "KISS (Keep It Simple, Stupid)"
category: "Software Engineering Process"
level: "Foundational"
---

Simplicity should be a key design goal. Avoid unnecessary complexity. The simplest solution that works is usually the best.

### Example

Don't build a microservices architecture for a blog with 100 users. A monolith with a single database is simpler, cheaper, and perfectly adequate.
