---
term: "Heap (Min-Heap / Max-Heap)"
category: "Data Structures"
level: "Intermediate"
---

A complete binary tree where the parent is always smaller (min-heap) or larger (max-heap) than its children. Root is the extreme value.

### Example

Min-heap: the smallest element is always at the top. Used in priority queues, Dijkstra's algorithm, and heap sort.
