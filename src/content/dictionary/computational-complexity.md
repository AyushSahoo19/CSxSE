---
term: "Computational Complexity"
category: "Math & Theory"
level: "Advanced"
---

Classifying problems by the resources (time, space) needed to solve them. Complexity classes: P, NP, NP-complete, NP-hard, PSPACE, EXPTIME.

### Example

Sorting is in P (polynomial time — efficient). Traveling Salesman decision version is NP-complete (no known efficient algorithm). This classification guides algorithm design.
