---
term: "Sprint"
category: "Software Engineering Process"
level: "Foundational"
---

A fixed time period (usually 1-4 weeks) in which a Scrum team completes a set of planned work. Ends with a potentially shippable product increment.

### Example

Sprint 7 (2 weeks): Implement user registration, login, and password reset. At the end, these features are working, tested, and deployable.
