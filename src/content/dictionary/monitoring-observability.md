---
term: "Monitoring & Observability"
category: "DevOps & Infrastructure"
level: "Intermediate"
---

Monitoring: watching predefined metrics (CPU, errors). Observability: understanding internal system state from external outputs (logs, metrics, traces). Three pillars.

### Example

Monitoring alerts you that error rate spiked. Observability helps you figure out WHY — trace a failing request through 5 services to find the broken one.
