---
term: "Quick Sort"
category: "Algorithms"
level: "Intermediate"
---

Pick a 'pivot', partition elements into less-than and greater-than groups, recursively sort each group. O(n log n) average, O(n²) worst.

### Example

Like organizing books — pick a random book, put shorter titles left and longer right. Repeat for each side. Fast in practice with good pivots.
