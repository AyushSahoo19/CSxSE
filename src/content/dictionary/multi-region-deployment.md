---
term: "Multi-Region Deployment"
category: "Cloud & Scalability"
level: "Advanced"
---

Deploying your application across multiple geographic regions for lower latency and higher availability. If one region goes down, others continue serving.

### Example

Deploy in US-East, EU-West, and AP-Southeast. Users are routed to the nearest region. If US-East has an outage, traffic shifts to EU-West automatically.
