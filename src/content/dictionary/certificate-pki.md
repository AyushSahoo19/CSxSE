term: "Certificate / PKI"
category: "Security"
level: "Advanced"
---

Digital certificates verify the identity of websites (and other entities). PKI (Public Key Infrastructure) is the system of certificate authorities (CAs) that issue and verify certificates.

### Example

When you visit google.com, your browser checks Google's SSL certificate, issued by a trusted CA. This proves you're talking to the real Google, not an impersonator.
