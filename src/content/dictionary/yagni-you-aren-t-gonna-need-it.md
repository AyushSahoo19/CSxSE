---
term: "YAGNI (You Aren't Gonna Need It)"
category: "Software Engineering Process"
level: "Foundational"
---

Don't build features or abstractions until you actually need them. Speculative generality leads to unused, complex code.

### Example

Don't build a plugin system, multi-tenancy, and internationalization for your MVP. Build what users need NOW. Add complexity when there's a real requirement.
