---
term: "Kruskal's / Prim's Algorithm"
category: "Algorithms"
level: "Advanced"
---

Find the Minimum Spanning Tree (MST) — connecting all nodes with minimum total edge weight. Kruskal's sorts edges; Prim's grows from a start node.

### Example

Laying fiber optic cable to connect 50 buildings with minimum total cable length. MST gives the cheapest network topology.
