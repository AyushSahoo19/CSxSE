---
term: "Scrum"
category: "Software Engineering Process"
level: "Intermediate"
---

An Agile framework with defined roles (Product Owner, Scrum Master, Dev Team), events (Sprint, Daily Standup, Review, Retro), and artifacts (Backlog, Board).

### Example

2-week sprint: team commits to 10 stories. Daily 15-min standup: what I did, what I'll do, any blockers. Sprint review: demo to stakeholders. Retro: how to improve.
