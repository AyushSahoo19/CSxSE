---
term: "Reinforcement Learning (RL)"
category: "AI / ML Foundations"
level: "Intermediate"
---

An agent learns by interacting with an environment, receiving rewards for good actions and penalties for bad ones. Learns optimal strategies through trial and error.

### Example

Training an AI to play chess: it plays thousands of games, receiving +1 for wins, -1 for losses. Over time, it learns winning strategies without being taught rules.
