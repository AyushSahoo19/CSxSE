---
term: "Kanban"
category: "Software Engineering Process"
level: "Intermediate"
---

A visual workflow management method using a board with columns (To Do, In Progress, Done). Limits work-in-progress. Pull-based, no fixed sprints.

### Example

A physical or digital board where cards move from left to right. WIP limit of 3 means only 3 items can be 'In Progress' simultaneously — prevents overload.
