---
term: "Recursion"
category: "Fundamentals"
level: "Intermediate"
---

A function that calls itself to solve a problem by breaking it into smaller identical sub-problems. Must have a base case to stop.

### Example

Calculating factorial: 5! = 5 × 4! = 5 × 4 × 3! ... Like Russian nesting dolls — each one opens to reveal a smaller version.
