---
term: "gRPC"
category: "Networking & Web"
level: "Advanced"
---

A high-performance RPC framework using HTTP/2 and Protocol Buffers. Supports streaming, is strongly typed, and is much faster than REST+JSON.

### Example

Microservices communicating internally use gRPC for speed — binary serialization (protobuf) is 10x smaller and faster than JSON. Used by Google, Netflix.
