---
term: "Cloud Computing"
category: "Cloud & Scalability"
level: "Foundational"
---

On-demand access to computing resources (servers, storage, databases, networking) over the internet. Pay for what you use. No physical hardware management.

### Example

Instead of buying $50,000 servers, rent them from AWS for $0.10/hour. Scale up during Black Friday, scale down after. AWS, GCP, Azure are the big three.
