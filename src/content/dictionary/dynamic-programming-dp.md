---
term: "Dynamic Programming (DP)"
category: "Algorithms"
level: "Intermediate"
---

Solve complex problems by breaking them into overlapping sub-problems, solving each only once, and storing results. Avoids redundant computation.

### Example

Fibonacci: fib(5) needs fib(4) and fib(3). fib(4) also needs fib(3). Without DP, you compute fib(3) twice. With DP, compute once, reuse.
