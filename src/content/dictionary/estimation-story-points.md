---
term: "Estimation (Story Points)"
category: "Software Engineering Process"
level: "Intermediate"
---

Assigning a relative effort score to user stories, usually using Fibonacci numbers (1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13). Measures complexity, not time.

### Example

'Login page' = 3 points (straightforward). 'Payment integration' = 13 points (complex, uncertain). Team velocity: we complete ~30 points per sprint.
