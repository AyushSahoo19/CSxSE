---
term: "Test Pyramid"
category: "Testing & QA"
level: "Intermediate"
---

A testing strategy: many fast unit tests (base), fewer integration tests (middle), fewest E2E tests (top). Balances speed, coverage, and confidence.

### Example

500 unit tests (run in 10 seconds), 50 integration tests (run in 2 minutes), 10 E2E tests (run in 10 minutes). Most bugs caught cheaply at the bottom.
