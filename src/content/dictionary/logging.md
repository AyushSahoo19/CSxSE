---
term: "Logging"
category: "DevOps & Infrastructure"
level: "Foundational"
---

Recording events, errors, and activities that occur during program execution. Essential for debugging and auditing.

### Example

logger.info('User 42 logged in from IP 192.168.1.5'). When something breaks at 3 AM, logs are your only witness. Use structured logging (JSON) for searchability.
