---
term: "Programming Paradigm"
category: "Programming Languages & Paradigms"
level: "Foundational"
---

A fundamental style or approach to programming. Different paradigms suit different problems. Most modern languages support multiple paradigms.

### Example

Imperative (how to do: C), Object-Oriented (things: Java), Functional (transforms: Haskell), Declarative (what to do: SQL), Logical (rules: Prolog).
