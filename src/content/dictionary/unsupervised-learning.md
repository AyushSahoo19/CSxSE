---
term: "Unsupervised Learning"
category: "AI / ML Foundations"
level: "Foundational"
---

Learning from unlabeled data — the model finds hidden patterns, structures, or groupings without being told what to look for.

### Example

Given 10,000 customer purchase histories with no labels, the model discovers 5 customer segments: 'budget shoppers', 'luxury buyers', 'tech enthusiasts', etc.
