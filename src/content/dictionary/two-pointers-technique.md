---
term: "Two Pointers Technique"
category: "Algorithms"
level: "Intermediate"
---

Use two pointers (indices) to traverse data from different positions or speeds. Solves many array/string problems in O(n) instead of O(n²).

### Example

Finding a pair that sums to a target in a sorted array: one pointer at start, one at end. Move inward based on current sum.
