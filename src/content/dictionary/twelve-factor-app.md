A methodology for building SaaS apps that are portable, scalable, and deployable on modern cloud platforms. 12 principles including config in env vars, stateless processes, etc.

### Example

Factor III: Config in environment variables (not hardcoded). Factor VI: Stateless processes. Factor IX: Fast startup and graceful shutdown.
