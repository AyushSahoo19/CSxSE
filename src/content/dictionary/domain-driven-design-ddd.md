---
term: "Domain-Driven Design (DDD)"
category: "System Design & Architecture"
level: "Advanced"
---

Designing software based on the business domain, using a ubiquitous language shared between developers and domain experts. Bounded contexts separate different domain models.

### Example

In e-commerce, 'Product' means different things to Catalog (name, description, images) and Warehouse (weight, dimensions, location). Each is a bounded context.
