---
term: "Public Key Infrastructure (PKI)"
category: "Cryptography & Web3"
level: "Advanced"
---

A set of roles, policies, hardware, software and procedures needed to create, manage, distribute, use, store and revoke digital certificates and manage public-key encryption.

### Example

The underlying trust system that allows your browser to verify that the SSL certificate served by `bank.com` is legitimate via Certificate Authorities.
