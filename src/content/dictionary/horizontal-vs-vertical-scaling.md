---
term: "Horizontal vs Vertical Scaling"
category: "System Design & Architecture"
level: "Intermediate"
---

Vertical: add more power to one machine (bigger CPU/RAM). Horizontal: add more machines. Horizontal is preferred for web-scale systems.

### Example

Vertical: upgrading from 8GB to 64GB RAM (has limits). Horizontal: adding 10 more servers behind a load balancer (nearly limitless).
