---
term: "Embedding"
category: "AI / ML Foundations"
level: "Intermediate"
---

Converting data (text, images) into dense numerical vectors that capture semantic meaning. Similar items have similar vectors.

### Example

'king' and 'queen' have similar embeddings. 'king' - 'man' + 'woman' ≈ 'queen'. Enables semantic search, recommendations, and clustering.
