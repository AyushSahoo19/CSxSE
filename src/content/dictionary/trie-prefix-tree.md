---
term: "Trie (Prefix Tree)"
category: "Data Structures"
level: "Intermediate"
---

A tree where each node represents a character, and paths from root to nodes form strings. Enables O(m) prefix-based search (m = key length).

### Example

Autocomplete — typing 'app' instantly finds 'apple', 'application', 'appetizer' by walking the trie's branches.
