---
term: "Greedy Algorithm"
category: "Algorithms"
level: "Intermediate"
---

Makes the locally optimal choice at each step, hoping to find the global optimum. Doesn't always work but is fast when applicable.

### Example

Making change with the fewest coins: always pick the largest coin that fits. $0.67 → 2 quarters + 1 dime + 1 nickel + 2 pennies. Works for US coins but not all coin systems.
