---
term: "MapReduce"
category: "Data Engineering & Big Data"
level: "Advanced"
---

A programming model for processing massively large datasets in parallel across a distributed cluster. It consists of a Map step (filtering and sorting) and a Reduce step (summary operation).

### Example

Counting word occurrences across 10,000 Wikipedia articles: The 'Map' step counts words within individual files, and the 'Reduce' step aggregates those counts globally.
