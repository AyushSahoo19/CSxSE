---
term: "Adjacency Matrix"
category: "Data Structures"
level: "Intermediate"
---

A 2D array representation of a graph where matrix[i][j] = 1 (or weight) if there's an edge from vertex i to j.

### Example

A 5-city map as a 5×5 grid. matrix[0][3] = 150 means it's 150km from city 0 to city 3. Fast lookup but wastes space for sparse graphs.
