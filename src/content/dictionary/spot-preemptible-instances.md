---
term: "Spot / Preemptible Instances"
category: "Cloud & Scalability"
level: "Advanced"
---

Discounted cloud compute instances that can be reclaimed by the provider with short notice. 60-90% cheaper than on-demand. Good for fault-tolerant workloads.

### Example

Run your batch data processing on spot instances — 70% cheaper. If instances are reclaimed, the job resumes on new instances. Not suitable for user-facing servers.
