---
term: "Gradient Descent"
category: "AI / ML Foundations"
level: "Intermediate"
---

An optimization algorithm that iteratively adjusts model parameters to minimize a loss function. Follows the steepest downward slope to find the minimum.

### Example

Imagine you're blindfolded on a hill. You feel the slope, step downhill, repeat. Eventually you reach the valley (minimum loss). Learning rate = step size.
