Probability: likelihood of events. Statistics: collecting, analyzing, and interpreting data. Foundation of ML, A/B testing, and decision-making.

### Example

A/B test: version A converts 4.2%, version B converts 4.8%. Is B actually better, or is it random chance? Statistics (p-value, confidence interval) answers this.
