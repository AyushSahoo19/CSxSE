---
term: "Algorithm"
category: "Algorithms"
level: "Foundational"
---

A step-by-step procedure to solve a specific problem in a finite number of steps. Must be unambiguous, finite, and produce correct output.

### Example

A recipe is an algorithm: Step 1: Boil water. Step 2: Add pasta. Step 3: Cook 10 min. Step 4: Drain. Clear, finite, deterministic.
