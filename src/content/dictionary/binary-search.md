---
term: "Binary Search"
category: "Algorithms"
level: "Foundational"
---

Search a SORTED array by repeatedly halving the search space. Compare middle element, go left if smaller, right if larger. O(log n).

### Example

Guessing a number 1-1000: 'Is it > 500?' 'Is it > 750?' — at most 10 guesses for 1000 numbers. Each guess eliminates half.
