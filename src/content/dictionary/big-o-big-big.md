---
term: "Big O / Big Ω / Big Θ"
category: "Algorithms"
level: "Intermediate"
---

Big O = upper bound (worst case). Big Ω = lower bound (best case). Big Θ = tight bound (exact growth rate). Big O is most commonly used.

### Example

Linear search: O(n) worst case, Ω(1) best case (found at first position), Θ(n) average case.
