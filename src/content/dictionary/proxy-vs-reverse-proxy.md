---
term: "Proxy vs Reverse Proxy"
category: "Networking & Web"
level: "Intermediate"
---

Forward proxy: acts on behalf of the CLIENT (hides client identity). Reverse proxy: acts on behalf of the SERVER (hides server identity, handles load balancing).

### Example

Forward: VPN hides your IP when browsing. Reverse: Nginx sits in front of your servers, distributing traffic and handling SSL. Users never see your actual servers.
